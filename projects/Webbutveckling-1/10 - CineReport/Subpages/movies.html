The Martian

About

Stranded on Mars, is not something you would want. That is what the head character Matt Damon has to deal with in this adventure space movie. While being stranded on Mars, Matt, or Astronaut Mark Watney, has to do everything to survive.

Actors

  • Matt Damon - As Mark Watney
  • The Space Crew
  • Chiwetel Ejiofor - As Vincent Kapoor
  • Jeff Daniels - As Teddy Sanders
